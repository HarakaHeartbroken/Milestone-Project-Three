The Iron Warriors are one of the 9 Traitor Legions of Chaos Space Marines that turned to the service of Chaos during the Horus Heresy and now fight to overthrow the Imperium of Man. The Iron Warriors, originally the IV Space Marine Legion, specialise in the breaking of sieges and assaults on static fortifications.
